We are a Robotics and Computer Vision research group at 
the [Georgia Tech Institute of Technology](http://www.gatech.edu). 
Our work is currently focused around using factor graphs in robotics and perception. 
We develop and maintain the [GTSAM sensor fusion library](https://gtsam.org).
